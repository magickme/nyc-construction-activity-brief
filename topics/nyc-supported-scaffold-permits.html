NYC Construction Activity Brief

NYC supported scaffold permit activity for weekly review.

The brief groups selected supported scaffold records from NYC DOB NOW public data into a small weekly CSV and Markdown package.

Who it helps

Scaffold contractors, site-access vendors, construction suppliers, and sales teams watching exterior and access-related work.

What you get

Work type, ZIP, borough, issued date, status, cost bucket, source link, permit ID, filing number, and short description.

Current sample

The first issue sample includes 24 supported scaffold rows from the 2026-06-01 to 2026-06-08 public-record pull.

Use case

Use the CSV to scan supported scaffold activity by ZIP, borough, and issued date before opening individual source records for closer review.

Boundary

No guaranteed leads. No owner names, applicant names, phone numbers, email addresses, or full street addresses are included. Source records can be incomplete, delayed, revised, duplicated, or mislabeled.

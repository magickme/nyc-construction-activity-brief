NYC Construction Activity Brief

NYC DOB permit monitoring for selected construction work types.

The NYC Weekly Construction Activity Brief turns selected DOB NOW public permit records into a simple weekly CSV and Markdown brief for territory review.

Who it helps

Construction-support vendors, specialty subcontractors, suppliers, and small teams that want a faster weekly review workflow.

What you get

Work type, ZIP, borough, issued date, status, cost bucket, source link, permit ID, filing number, and short description.

Current sample

The first issue sample includes 192 public preview rows from the 2026-06-01 to 2026-06-08 pull.

Use case

Use this product when DOB search screens are too slow for weekly review. The files are designed for quick filtering by work type, territory, and issued date before manual source checks.

Boundary

No guaranteed leads. No owner names, applicant names, phone numbers, email addresses, or full street addresses are included. Source records can be incomplete, delayed, revised, duplicated, or mislabeled.

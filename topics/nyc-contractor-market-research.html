NYC Construction Activity Brief

NYC contractor market research from public permit activity.

The brief gives small construction businesses a source-linked weekly view of selected public permit rows.

Who it helps

Contractors, vendors, and suppliers checking which selected work types are appearing in the current sample.

What you get

Work type, ZIP, borough, issued date, status, cost bucket, source link, permit ID, filing number, and short description.

Current sample

The first issue sample includes Manhattan and Brooklyn rows across five selected ZIP codes.

Use case

Use it to prepare a research session, compare territories, or decide whether the current weekly package is relevant to your market.

Boundary

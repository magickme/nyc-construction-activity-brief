NYC Construction Activity Brief

NYC construction sales prospecting with public permit signals.

The brief gives sales teams a small source-linked activity file for selected public permit rows.

Who it helps

Sales teams at construction-support vendors that already verify source records before outreach.

What you get

Work type, ZIP, borough, issued date, status, cost bucket, source link, permit ID, filing number, and short description.

Current sample

The current public preview includes seven selected work types across Manhattan and Brooklyn ZIP codes.

Use case

Use the file to decide which records deserve manual review first. It does not include contact enrichment, outreach automation, or lead scoring.

Boundary

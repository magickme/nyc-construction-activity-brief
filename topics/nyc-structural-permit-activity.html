NYC Construction Activity Brief

NYC structural permit activity for weekly screening.

The brief packages selected structural permit activity from NYC DOB NOW public records into buyer-safe fields with source links kept visible.

Who it helps

Structural contractors, construction suppliers, inspection-adjacent vendors, and sales teams reviewing active project signals.

What you get

Work type, ZIP, borough, issued date, status, cost bucket, source link, permit ID, filing number, and short description.

The first issue sample includes 22 structural rows from the 2026-06-01 to 2026-06-08 public-record pull.

Boundary
